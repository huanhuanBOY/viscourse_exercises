As the coronavirus outbreak continues to spread across China and other countries in the world, the global confirmed cases now exceed 78,000 and the global death toll is at least 2458. All sectors of the society in China and the world have been working together to fight for the new coronavirus. Among them, researchers and social media try to utilize visualization to inform the viscourse_exercises about the virus-related data.

In the following, there are four visualizations to show the change of the death toll and other related information.

  • Please read them carefully and summarize the insights you find, strengths, weakness, and the way to improve for this kind of time-series data for each visualization.
  • Please also rank the four visualizations according to your analysis.
  • Please submit your pdf file through Canvas.
